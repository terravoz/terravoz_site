About

Terravoz is an open-source tech shop whose innovative voice, SMS, and web-based solutions foster democratic participation and community empowerment worldwide.

Our mission is to meet the information needs of the most underserved individuals — 10 to 17 million in the U.S. and 5.6 billion worldwide — who rely on basic mobile phones as their main form of technological access.

Services

Terravoz's products are built on our extensive collection of simple, ready-made open source modules that bring the power of voice, touch-phone telephony, and SMS to connect technologically-challenged communities to the Internet.

Key benefits of our unique, non-proprietary products include:

• Easy installation and configuration.
• No additional programming needed.
• Easy to customize. Only use the features you need.
• The ability to unify web, email, voice, and SMS.
• Sustainably develop scalable projects
• Users don't have to have a costly data plan
• Support from a community of open-source developers
• For developers: our VoIP Drupal platform is built on a visual programming language for fast prototyping

Products Customized for Mobile Phones
